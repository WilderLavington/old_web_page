My Academic Life Thus Far

After completing my degree at the University of Colorado Boulder, I was accepted to the Department of Computer Science at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver BC. My current research focuses on creating more principled gradient based policy optimization algorithms in reinforcement learning. I believe that by viewing reinforcement learning as probabalistic inference, we can improve our understanding of optimization within RL, especially within sequential and multi-objective tasks.

Mountaineering

For anyone who has visited Boulder, my disposition and hobbies should come as no surprise. Now that I am in Vancouver, I have Whistler and Squamish at my disposal for all the world class climbing, biking, and skiing I could ask for!
